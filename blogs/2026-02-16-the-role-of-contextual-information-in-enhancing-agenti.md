When AI agents access high-quality contextual data, they deliver significantly better results. Quantexa's experiments showed that contextualized data delivered around 40% better risk mitigation than un-contextualized data. Testing three AI agents—a research agent, insurance agent, and banking agent—the company found that access to its Agent Gateway consistently improved accuracy and risk detection, letting agents identify complex fraud patterns that would otherwise be missed.
