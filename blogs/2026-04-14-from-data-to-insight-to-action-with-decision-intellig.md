The article explains how Quantexa's Decision Intelligence Platform bridges data, insights, and action through three phases: building a trusted data foundation using entity resolution, revealing hidden insights via contextual knowledge graphs, and enabling human-AI collaboration across three decision-making levels (support, augmentation, and automation). It emphasizes that accurate decisions depend first on trusted data and real-world context rather than speed or advanced models alone.
